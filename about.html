About Me

As a dedicated medical professional with a background in Community Medicine, I am passionate about transforming public health through data-driven insights and evidence-based approaches. My journey from an MBBS graduate to a postgraduate at Maulana Azad Medical College has been driven by a core belief that focusing on prevention and health promotion is the way forward. My expertise spans multiple critical domains: epidemiology, biostatistics, healthcare management, and data science. I leverage analytical skills to translate complex healthcare data into meaningful insights. With experience in medical research, peer reviews, and conference presentations, I am committed to advancing our understanding of public health challenges. My academic and research focus has mainly centered on data science in public health, mental health, and palliative care. I have contributed to the field through peer-reviewed publications and national and international conference presentations. As I complete my MD in Community Medicine, I am driven to bridge research, data analysis, and practical healthcare interventions.
